INSTEAD OF TRYING TO BE DIFFERENT
BE MEANINGFUL

The common brand are always trying to find new ways to standout from the competitors instead of looking for the essence inside them, the thing that makes every Brand unique from the rest.

To find what makes a Brand unique and truly different, we need to answer these questions before, during and after the creation of the Brand.

1. ¿Who are we? We should perfectly know the essence of the Brand, the products and services.

2. ¿Where are we? We should acknowledge the objectives of our clients and the competition.

3. ¿What do we do? We need to understand the real needs and problems that the Brand will be able to resolve.

4. ¿How do we do it? Knowing what things of the user experience of the Brand is different from the rest.

5. ¿To whom it may concern? We should define goal public (age, sociallanguage, user behavior, media and social media

6. ¿Why do we do it? We contribute creating the experience of the users.

In this way, your project becomes exceptional in 3 different levels

Even more, this will help us understand towards where are we going and which will be the right direction to take.
