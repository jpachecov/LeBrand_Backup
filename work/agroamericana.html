Project.

Agroamericana Corp.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for the agribusiness holding with offices in Mexico, California and New York.
Agroamericana works within the 4 sectors of the Agro-business: the farming, harvest, distribution, and marketing of agricultural products. Le Brand had the challenge of creating a unique identity for this international holding.
Le Brand created the branding, web design, and communication strategies needed in order to help introduce the Agroamericana brand into North American markets and connect them with Latin American agribusiness companies.
