Project.

FIGHTER TOWN BREWING CO.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for the Fighter Town Brewing Co. In 2016 will be San Diego’s primer contract brewer.
Fighter Town is a local Californian contract craft brewery that assists craft breweries in reaching their potential through strategic partnership and lucrative tasting rooms. Fighter Town approach Le Brand with the challenge of creating an identity that could stand out and act as an example for other local craft breweries competing against the established national brands that control more than the 90% of the national market.
Le Brand responded to the call of duty for this important mission. The victory of the local allies against the commercial monster brands depended on the creation of a very powerful weapon: The Fighter Town company’s branding.
Through many strategic reunions with the commanders of this mission, extensive marketing research, and some true Californian inspiration such as the Top Gun movie, Le Brand lead this amazing contract craft beer project to victory. Le Brand delivered the identity and image required for the battlefield.
The illustration and the original typography design created by Le Brand were accepted with high praise by the investors and the users. Right now Fighter Town is under construction and will soon be ready to start expanding from California to therest of the United States and the world.
