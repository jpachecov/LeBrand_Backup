Project.

Centennial Organics.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for a Californian new agribusiness company.

Centennial Organics is a company from Agrobusiness Holding of Agroamericana. This company specializes in the sale, distribution, and marketing of organic Californian produce. The challenge of this project was to create an identity for the company that could compete with the already established brands on the market such as Dole, Chiquita, and Driscoll’s in Whole Food Markets. Vigorous marketing research and assessment of consumer’s behavior approaches helped Le Brand determine the most accurate naming for the project. This research also lead to the development of a very well detailed illustration concept that strongly imparts the meaning of a traditional American family farms company.
