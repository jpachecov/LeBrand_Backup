Project.

Sunslice LLC.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for a Mexican-Californian fresh produce agribusiness brand.
Sunslice’s consumers’ desires were critical in determining the process of creating a brand for the company. While developing branding for Sunslice, Le Brand was asked to create very high quality visuals that would meet the high expectations of millennial consumers. Sunslice was looking for branding that would seem friendly and sustainable, and would represent the company with a healthy personality. At the same time, Sunslice wanted the designs to be forceful, sober, and minimalistic. Le Brand met these demands with enthusiasm and Sunslice was not disappointed.
