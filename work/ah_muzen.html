Project.

Ah Muzen Kab.S.A de C.V

Identity + Branding for a Honey Bee’s company in the state of Quintana Roo, Mexico. In 2014 the Honey Bee company Ah Muzen Kab expressed a desire to have a solid and structured identity and brand for the company’s objectives.

Ah Muzen Kab reached out to Le Brand for the next challenge in the process of their development: configuring the identity and branding for the unique and organic honey bee of the Mayan jungles from Quintana Roo that would be commercialized and exported to different countries all around the world such as Japan, Germany, and the United States. Le Brand used marketing research to create a unique identity for the company and to develop branding that met the quality required to be competitive in international markets, without compromising the regional aspects of the project and the company. Le Brand helped Ah Muzen Kab foster and fortify an identity that reflects the culture of the Mayan people and their traditional beekeeping processes through a carefully designed illustration and well-selected typography.

The new image was quickly adopted by the project and is currently being used as the brand identity for the products, packages, and social networks of Ah Muzen Kab.
